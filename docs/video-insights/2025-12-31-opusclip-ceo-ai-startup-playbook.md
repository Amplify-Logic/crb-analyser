## Strategic Implications for CRB Analyser

### What We're Doing Right:
1. **Vertical focus** on service businesses (boring but lucrative)
2. **Industry KB** as proprietary data that compounds
3. **Three Options framework** = workflow ownership, not just AI output
4. **Expertise system** that learns from each analysis

### Gaps to Address:
1. **Distribution channel** - still unclear, need to define in Q1
2. **Manual validation** - should we create 10-20 free reports for enterprise targets?
3. **Segment ruthlessness** - are we narrow enough? "Marketing agencies" vs "7-15 person marketing agencies scaling past $1M ARR"

### Key Reframe:
Position as **"Consultant-as-Software"** not "AI analysis tool." Our benchmark for pricing and value is $5-20K consultant engagements, not $99/mo AI tools.
